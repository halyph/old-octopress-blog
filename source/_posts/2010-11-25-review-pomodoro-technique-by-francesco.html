--- layout: post title: 'Review: The Pomodoro Technique by Francesco Cirillo' date: '2010-11-25T01:48:00.003+02:00' author: Orest Ivasiv tags: - Pomodoro - Books - Time Management modified_time: '2010-12-10T18:45:37.996+02:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-7372777165432727866.post-6640819701160676364 blogger_orig_url: http://www.halyph.com/2010/11/review-pomodoro-technique-by-francesco.html --- Some time ago I read this book "The Pomodoro Technique" by Francesco Cirillo
I have to say that it's really useful and super fast introduction for this particular time management technique. I highly recommend it for everybody interested in Pomodoro.
I don't want to analyze main chapters because you just have to read them all.
Of cause you read only about basic approaches and you have to adapt some of them. It's general practice. There is no single way to do the job, there is only recommended way to organize you work time. You have to start applying Pomodoro iteratively and make correction to "your" technique after each working day.

Have fun with Pomodoro.
